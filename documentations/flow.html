🔀 Flow Bisnis GoMad

Diagram interaktif alur bisnis GoMad dari registrasi hingga settlement.

1. Flow Registrasi & Verifikasi

                graph TD
                    subgraph Customer
                        C[Registrasi Customer] --> C1[Pilih Role: Customer]
                        C1 --> C2[Isi Nama, Email, HP, Password]
                        C2 --> C3[Atau Google Login]
                        C3 --> C4[Akun Aktif]
                        C4 --> C5[Redirect ke /customer/setup]
                        C5 --> C6[Wajib isi WhatsApp]
                    end

                    subgraph Agency
                        A[Registrasi Agency] --> A1[Pilih Role: Agency]
                        A1 --> A2[Isi data & upload dokumen]
                        A2 --> A3[Ajukan Verifikasi]
                        A3 --> A4{Pending}
                        A4 --> A5[Admin Approve]
                        A5 --> A6[Agency Aktif]
                        A4 --> A7[Admin Reject]
                        A7 --> A8[Setup Ulang]
                    end

                    subgraph Warung
                        W[Registrasi Warung] --> W1[Pilih Role: Warung]
                        W1 --> W2[Isi data & PIN]
                        W2 --> W3[Setup Profil]
                        W3 --> W4[Verifikasi Admin]
                        W4 --> W5[Bisa Terima Pembayaran]
                    end

                    subgraph Driver
                        D[Driver] --> D1[Tidak bisa daftar sendiri]
                        D1 --> D2[Agency buat akun driver]
                        D2 --> D3[Driver login]
                    end

2. Flow Booking & Pembayaran

                graph TD
                    Customer[Customer Login] --> Search[Cari Jadwal]
                    Search --> Book[Klik Booking]
                    Book --> Step1[Pilih Kota Penjemputan]
                    Step1 --> Step2[Isi Alamat Penjemputan]
                    Step2 --> Step3[Pilih Kota Tujuan]
                    Step3 --> Step4[Isi Data Penumpang]
                    Step4 --> Create[Klik Buat Booking]
                    Create --> Detail[Halaman Detail Booking]
                    Detail --> Payment{Pilih Pembayaran}

                    Payment --> Online[Bayar Online Midtrans]
                    Online --> Popup[Popup Midtrans]
                    Popup --> Paid[Booking PAID]

                    Payment --> Warung[Bayar di Warung]
                    Warung --> Code[Generate Kode Bayar]
                    Code --> Show[Tampil Kode + Peta]
                    Show --> Confirm[Warung Konfirmasi]
                    Confirm --> Paid

                    Payment --> COD[Bayar COD]
                    COD --> Validasi[Validasi COD]
                    Validasi --> Hold[Hold Deposit Agency]
                    Hold --> DriverConfirm[Driver Konfirmasi COD]
                    DriverConfirm --> Paid

                    Paid --> ETicket[E-Ticket Tersedia]

3. Flow Perjalanan (Driver)

                graph TD
                    Agency[Agency] --> Start[Klik Mulai Jadwal]
                    Start --> Notif[Notifikasi ke Driver]
                    Notif --> Driver[Driver Login]
                    Driver --> List[Lihat Daftar Booking]

                    List --> Pickup[Jemput Penumpang]
                    Pickup --> Dropoff[Antar ke Tujuan]
                    Dropoff --> COD{Cek COD?}
                    COD -->|Ya| ConfirmCOD[Konfirmasi COD]
                    COD -->|Tidak| Finish[Booking Selesai]
                    ConfirmCOD --> Finish

                    Finish --> AllDone[Semua Booking Selesai]
                    AllDone --> Complete[Klik Selesaikan Perjalanan]
                    Complete --> Release[Release Funds ke Wallet Agency]

4. Flow Pembayaran Cash (Warung)

                graph TD
                    Customer[Customer dengan Kode Bayar] --> Warung[Warung Login]
                    Warung --> InputKode[Masukkan Kode Bayar]
                    InputKode --> Validasi{Valid?}
                    Validasi -->|Ya| InputPIN[Masukkan PIN]
                    InputPIN --> Verifikasi{Verifikasi PIN}
                    Verifikasi -->|Cocok| Confirm[Klik Konfirmasi Pembayaran]
                    Confirm --> Update[Update Database]
                    Update --> Notif[Notifikasi WhatsApp ke Customer]
                    Update --> ETicket[E-Ticket Tersedia]

                    Validasi -->|Tidak| Error1[Tampil Error]
                    Verifikasi -->|Tidak| Error2[Tampil Error]

5. Flow COD

                graph TD
                    Customer[Pilih COD] --> Validasi{Cek Kelayakan}
                    Validasi -->|Ya| Book[Booking CONFIRMED]
                    Book --> Hold[Hold COD Balance]
                    Hold --> Day[Hari H]
                    Day --> Pickup[Driver Jemput]
                    Pickup --> Pay[Customer Bayar CASH]
                    Pay --> Drop[Driver Antar ke Tujuan]
                    Drop --> Confirm[Driver Konfirmasi COD]
                    Confirm --> Release[Release COD Hold]
                    Release --> Paid[Booking PAID]

6. Flow Transfer Penumpang

                graph TD
                    Agency[Agency] --> Transfer[Klik Transfer pada Jadwal]
                    Transfer --> Select[Centang Booking]
                    Select --> Search[Cari Mobil Tujuan]
                    Search --> List[Tampil Daftar Jadwal Tersedia]
                    List --> Choose[Pilih Jadwal Tujuan]
                    Choose --> Internal{Internal Transfer?}
                    Internal -->|Ya| Approve[Approved]
                    Internal -->|Tidak| External[External Transfer PENDING]
                    External --> Wait[Menunggu Approval Agency Tujuan]
                    Wait -->|Approve| Approve
                    Wait -->|Reject| Reject[Reject]
                    Approve --> Move[Booking Dipindahkan]
                    Move --> Notif[Notifikasi ke Customer]

7. Flow Wallet & Withdrawal

                graph TD
                    Complete[Booking COMPLETED] --> Release[Release Funds]
                    Release --> Wallet[Wallet Agency]
                    Wallet --> Available[Saldo Tersedia]
                    Wallet --> Pending[Saldo Pending]
                    Wallet --> Deposit[Saldo Deposit]

                    Available --> Withdrawal[Ajukan Withdrawal]
                    Withdrawal --> Check{Total Withdrawal}
                    Check -->|< 5 Juta| Auto[Auto Approve]
                    Check -->|≥ 5 Juta| Manual[Pending Admin]
                    Auto --> Process[Processing]
                    Manual --> Approve[Admin Approve]
                    Manual --> Reject[Admin Reject]
                    Approve --> Process
                    Reject --> Return[Dana Dikembalikan]
                    Process --> Complete2[Withdrawal COMPLETED]

                    Deposit --> TopUp[Top Up Deposit]
                    TopUp --> Bayar[Bayar via Midtrans]
                    Bayar --> Add[Deposit Bertambah]

8. Flow Settlement Warung

                graph TD
                    Cron[Senin 00:01] --> Generate[Generate Settlement]
                    Generate --> Check{Cek Transaksi}
                    Check -->|Ada| Create[Buat Settlement]
                    Check -->|Tidak| Skip[Skip]

                    Create --> StatusPENDING[Status PENDING]
                    StatusPENDING --> Bayar[Warung Bayar via Midtrans]
                    Bayar --> StatusPAID[Status PAID]
                    StatusPAID --> Verify[Admin Verifikasi]
                    Verify --> Lunas[Status LUNAS]

                    StatusPENDING --> Overdue[> 7 Hari]
                    Overdue --> StatusOVERDUE[Status OVERDUE]

9. Flow Refund & Pembatalan

                graph TD
                    Cancel[Customer Batalkan Booking] --> Status{Cek Status}
                    Status -->|PENDING| CancelImmed[Booking CANCELLED]
                    Status -->|CONFIRMED| CancelImmed
                    Status -->|PAID| CheckTime{Cek Keberangkatan}
                    CheckTime -->|> 24 Jam| Hitung[Hitung Biaya Pembatalan]
                    CheckTime -->|≤ 24 Jam| NotAllowed[Tidak Bisa Cancel]

                    Hitung --> Refund[Proses Refund]
                    Refund --> Midtrans[Refund via Midtrans]
                    Midtrans --> Berhasil[Refund Berhasil]
                    Midtrans --> Gagal[Refund Gagal]
                    Gagal --> Admin[Manual Refund oleh Admin]
                    Berhasil --> BookingCancelled[Booking CANCELLED]
                    Admin --> BookingCancelled

10. Flow Referral

                graph TD
                    A[Customer A] --> Code[Punya Kode Referral]
                    Code --> Share[Bagikan ke Teman]
                    Share --> B[Customer B Daftar]
                    B --> First[Customer B Booking Pertama]
                    First --> Success[Pembayaran Sukses]
                    Success --> Trigger[Trigger Referral Reward]
                    Trigger --> Check{Cek Transaksi}
                    Check --> Diskon[Hitung Diskon]
                    Diskon --> Promo[Buat Promo REFERRAL]
                    Promo --> A2[Customer A Dapat Diskon]
                    A2 --> Use[Pakai di Booking Berikutnya]
                    Success --> Track[ReferralTracking: is_successful = true]
